About Samprit Bose

Hi, I’m Samprit — a curious mind who loves turning ideas into interactive, good-looking websites. I work with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, SQL, and Excel, building everything from clean, functional front-ends to data-driven solutions. Right now, I’m sharpening my skills in responsive design, modern JS features, and the fascinating world of data analytics and generative AI.

When I’m not coding, you’ll probably find me exploring tech trends, reading up on design inspiration, or getting lost in a good puzzle — whether it’s a tricky algorithm or a stubborn bug in my code. I also enjoy working on personal projects, like a book inventory system, a sales dashboard, and other experiments that combine creativity with problem-solving.

My hobbies keep me balanced — I like music, exploring new places, and a bit of fitness training to counter the hours spent in front of a screen.

Hailing from Konnagar, West Bengal, I am driven by a desire to create beautiful, functional, and user-friendly digital experiences. My goal is to become a data analyst who’s not just fluent in the language of numbers, but can make those numbers speak in a way that inspires decisions.
